# omi

- Source: [BasedHardware/omi](https://github.com/BasedHardware/omi)
- Category: Ambient multimodal assistant platform
- Snapshot stars (2026-04-17): 9,584
- License: MIT
- Language: Dart (plus mixed stack)

## What it gives you

A broad platform for screen/audio capture, transcription, summaries, and memory-driven assistant workflows across devices.

## Why this matters for Sol-HQ

- Inspiration for multimodal agent UX.
- Potential pattern source for meeting intelligence and ops assistants.
- Useful examples of desktop/mobile integration.

## Risks and caveats

- Large system scope and operational complexity.
- Privacy and consent requirements are substantial for capture workflows.

## Recommended usage

- Treat as architecture inspiration, not immediate full-stack adoption.
- Reuse only tightly scoped components where policy and compliance are clear.

## First implementation step

Extract one narrow use case: meeting summary + action item generation for internal team workflows.
